About Us!

Connecting India to Resources

The Supply Chain Net brings India’s first Supply Chain Networking platform where Providers of Quality Services can connect to Seekers of such Quality Services directly. The platform is not limited to particular categories of services but is open to all the services which are related to Supply Chain Industry.

The Supply Chain Net is a brain child of three colleagues from the Supply Chain Industry who understand the hardships of service providers and service seekers that they face in the Supply Chain Industry in the modern India.

How does Supply Chain Net Work?

The user begins by selecting if he/she is a service seeker or the service provider and then registers as either one of those.
After registration, each user is taken to their dashboard where they need to update their information in the profile. This information helps us to identify you and make sure you are who you say you are.
After the profile update, the users can either post a service or search for services!

The Supply Chain network is India’s first supply chain networking hub which connects service providers and service seekers alike and try to bridge the gap of supply-demand in the economics of supply chain services.
